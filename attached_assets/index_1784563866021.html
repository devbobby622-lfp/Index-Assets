What this is

Fan-built. Free. Forever.

Against Gravity changed Rec Room a lot over the years. We wanted to preserve the version that felt most like home — the 2020 build with its original rooms, community energy, and features that got removed.

This is not official. It is run by fans, for fans, completely free. We just want to keep playing the game we loved.

What works

Everything that mattered.

The core of the 2020 experience is working. Here is what is live.

Authentication

Accounts work. Log in with your original credentials and your identity saves across sessions.

Custom rooms

The Maker Pen is fully supported. Build, save, and share your rooms the old way.

Player profiles

Your bio, outfit, and stats all persist. Profiles work the same as they did in 2020.

Stable connection

The server sends a heartbeat every 30 seconds to keep your session alive and lag-free.

Self-hosted

We run everything ourselves. No dependency on Against Gravity.

Original client

Point your 2020 mobile client at our server and you are straight back in the game.

Ready to jump back in?

You need the original 2020 Rec Room client. Once you have it, point it at our server and you are done.

01

Get the original client

Find the original 2020 Rec Room APK or IPA. Do not use the current version from the app store.

02

Redirect traffic

Patch your hosts file or use our redirect tool to send rec.net traffic to our server.

03

Log in and play

Open the app, log in, and your rooms, profile, and friends will all be there.
